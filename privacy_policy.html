Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

This policy applies to all Axeris Games products, games, and related services.

This Privacy Policy explains how Axeris Games (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our games and services (collectively, the “Services”). By using the Services, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device & App Information: device model, OS version, app version, language, and approximate location derived from IP.
  • Identifiers: platform identifiers (e.g., Google Play Games / Apple Game Center) used to link cloud saves and gameplay progress.
  • Gameplay & Analytics: anonymous or pseudonymous gameplay events and performance metrics (e.g., sessions, crashes) to improve the Services.
  • Advertising Data: advertising identifiers and ad interaction data (e.g., impressions) when ads are enabled, depending on consent and region.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (including saving/loading progress where cloud saves are used).
  • Maintain, debug, and improve stability and performance (analytics, crash diagnostics).
  • Show ads and measure their performance where applicable (and only as allowed by local laws and consent).
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

3. Data Storage & Security

Where cloud features are enabled, game data may be stored on third-party cloud infrastructure (such as Google Firebase). Data transmission is typically encrypted in transit. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted service providers only to operate the Services, such as:

  • Analytics / Cloud Save: Google Firebase (e.g., Analytics, Crash reporting, Cloud Firestore) when used by a game.
  • Advertising partners: providers used for in-game ads (for example, Google AdMob, Unity Ads, AppLovin/LevelPlay) where enabled.

Specific partners can vary per game and per platform. If a game does not use ads or analytics, those partners are not used in that game.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • Personalized Ads: you can usually opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Data Requests: you may request access, correction, or deletion of data associated with you, where applicable.
  • Consent: in regions requiring consent (e.g., EEA/UK), consent prompts may be shown by the app or an ad SDK.

To submit a request, email us at axerisgames@gmail.com.

6.Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

7. International Transfers

Depending on your location, your information may be processed on servers located in other countries. When required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page and the “Last Updated” date will change. Continued use of the Services after updates means you accept the updated policy.
